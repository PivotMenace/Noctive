About Venus!

In 2019, I met two of my closest friends on a gaming platform that no longer exists. What stayed with us was never the interface. It was the late-night conversations, the familiarity, and the real connection built across time zones.

Noctive started as an idea in one of my best friend's bedrooms. It was the kind of idea that could have stayed just a conversation, but it kept pushing me forward and reminding me that if there is a will, there is a way.

When that original platform shut down, the friendships remained. Noctive was built to preserve that feeling: a place where people can post without overthinking, share a win, start a conversation at 2AM, and feel like someone is genuinely there.

Project Status

Early-stage front-end prototype. Backend and controlled onboarding in progress.

Core Features
Tag-Based Profiles - Identity-driven discovery.
Focused Hubs - Structured spaces for games and discussions.

Post Feed - Share clips and ideas with earned visibility.
Recognition System - Badges for contribution and consistency.
Noctive Direction
